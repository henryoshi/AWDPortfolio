# AWD Portfolio — Project Brief

## Core Directives

- DO NOT CHANGE THE ACTUAL ENGLISH CONTENT, only the code surrounding it
- Priority is **artistic and emotional impact**, not technical efficiency or best practices
- This is a website

## Vision

A reflection-heavy, interwoven website that approaches English as a medium of expression. The portfolio looks back at the thought process of learning to write — reflections are time-stamped. Structure and form are intentional, used to convey meaning.

Contents will include:
- The three course reflections
- The midterm (including the "creature" paragraph)
- Snippets of pieces that "strike" the author
- Novel reflection spackle interwoven between previous excerpts — bastardizing, mocking, and enjoying re-reading prior attempts
- Peer comment feedback and responses woven in
- Possible engineering/social justice content if it fits organically
- Recognition of failures and future growth opportunities, not just successes

Tone: melodramatic, emotional, spiraling, honest. A break-up is in the mix. No apologies withheld.

## Technical Wishlist

### Audio
- Music transitions as the user scrolls through sections
- Only seconds of a song may play — that is enough
- Song selection is intentional and curated by the author
- Sourcing: attempting to rip actual artist MP3s (torrents if needed); fallback is copyright-free Pixabay SFX/ambience

### Backgrounds
- Custom backgrounds that change with scroll/section
- Ideas so far:
  - Dirty, overgrown, crumbling brick wall
  - Curtains on fire splashed with wine
  - Pitch black where text contrast is nearly impossible to read
  - Fiery ruined city (paired with *Conquest* by The White Stripes — pieces on the industrial-military complex)
- Theme word: **Metamorphosis** — backgrounds should feel like transformation

### Animations
- "Snazzy animations" — no promises, but aspirational
- Emotion and artistry over polish
