Its funny. Talking with some of my friends about this project they kept saying the same thing. "Why are you trying so hard on this." It's a fair question. A sentiment I might have shared a year or two ago.

Its funny. Reading back my discussion pieces. I am not sure I like most of them. And I like that. A lot. I like being at odds with my past-self. He was an idiot. I am a moron. We are the same but I am better. I hope.

Its funny. I am not prideful, but I want to take pride. To be proud of what I do. To make something of my own. To know every nook and cranny. To sit back and smile.

Its funny. The things we hold so paramount. The same things we forget in months. The things we are blind to until too late. The things we were wiser to when we were young.

Its funny. How narcissistic I feel. How guilt fills me when I express myself. How I crumple in the face of conflict. I hate using the word I. I use it so much. Its not even a word.

Its funny. How we grieve. How we grow. How we dream. How we regret. How we fear. How we hope. How we love. How we hate.

At the least its interesting.
